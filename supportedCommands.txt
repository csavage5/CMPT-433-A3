Volume Control
- Volume up: "vol u [amount]"
- Volume down: "vol d [amount]"
- Get current volume: "q vol"

Tempo Control
- Tempo up: "tempo u [amount]"
- Tempo down: "tempo d [amount]"
- Get current tempo: "q tempo"

Sound Control
- Play specific drum: "p drum [hh|snare|bass]"

Beat Control
- Play specific beat: "p beat [0|1|2]"

General Status
- Get uptime: "q uptime"
